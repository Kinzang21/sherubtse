Sherubtse College, Bhutan's first institution for higher education, was established in 1966 as a secondary school. In 1983, it was upgraded to a degree college affiliated with the University of Delhi, India. Located in Kanglung, Trashigang, Sherubtse is now part of the Royal University of Bhutan. The college is known for its scenic campus in eastern Bhutan and offers diverse undergraduate programs in arts, science, and commerce . It is celebrated for fostering academic excellence and leadership development, contributing significantly to Bhutan's education and human resource development.
