About Sherubtse

Sherubtse College is Bhutan's oldest higher educational institution, dedicated to nurturing the education of Bhutanese students. It is a member of the national university system in Bhutan, which comprises 10 colleges. The college warmly welcomes new students each academic year.

Vision

An internationally recognized institute in Liberal Arts and Sciences with an emphasis on GNH value based learning

Core Values

Professionalism
Innovation
Passion for Learning
Sense of Ownership
Community Services
Academic Excellence

Mission

To provide GNH inspired quality tertiary education in Liberal Arts and Sciences. To contribute to the development of knowledge-based society through knowledge creation, sharing and advocacy
